The Problem

Plastic Cutlery is Killing Our Planet

Every year, billions of plastic utensils end up in landfills and oceans. They take 500+ years to decompose, releasing toxic chemicals into our soil and water. The dining industry is one of the largest contributors to single-use plastic waste.

Our Process

From Grain to
Your Table

Every piece of EcoBite cutlery goes through a meticulous 9-step process to ensure premium quality and sustainability.

Step 01

Grain Selection

We source the finest organic wheat, rice, and millet flour from certified sustainable farms.

Step 02

Grinding

Grains are precision-ground to achieve the perfect texture for molding and durability.

Step 03

Mixing

Precisely measured natural ingredients are blended to create our proprietary grain formula.

Step 04

Dough Preparation

The mixture is kneaded into a pliable dough, tested for consistency and quality.

Step 05

Molding

Precision molds shape the dough into plates, bowls, cups, forks, and spoons.

Step 06

Baking

Each piece is baked at optimal temperature for strength, texture, and food safety.

Step 07

Cooling

Controlled cooling locks in the structure and ensures dimensional stability.

Step 08

Quality Inspection

Every piece is inspected for durability, finish, and compliance with food safety standards.

Step 09

Eco Packaging

Packaged in compostable materials, ready to ship to your door with zero plastic.
